About Amplio

At IMG, feedback is taken positively.

The main goal of everyone at IMG is to learn something new everyday and make the lives of IITians in Roorkee better in the process. We apply everything we've learnt and know in making new and useful apps that add a nifty little tool that touches and enriches lives.

The only way we know our apps are still relevant and that our work is not misaligned with our own ideals is through valuable input from the people to whom we cater with these applications. Thus Amplio was born out of a necessity: a necessity to know what was lacking and what needed to be done to improve the technological state of the institute from the only credible voice on the matter: the students themselves.

Amplio allows the users to post feedback on every aspect of the functioning of IMG. From bugs in software to feature demands, all sorts of feedback posted on Amplio are reviewed by the right IMGians for the job, the status of the fix is updated on the site and after it is fixed, the issue is closed off.

The most voted feedback and comments rise to the top, so that they can be brought before the people who made the app or have a fair idea of the machinery inside. They make it a priority to fix crtical bugs and add features by popular demand.

So go ahead. Speak your mind. Who knows, your idea combined with the power of IMG may revolutionize the way people live in IIT-R!
